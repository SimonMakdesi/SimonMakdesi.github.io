Project

You gotta be rifting me

Level Designer

This game was a school Project. I had several assignments such as level design, asset artist, world builder, sound design, animator and producer. My main role was level design.

Project Summary

A puzzle game in which the player possesses the power to open rifts on walls in order to solve puzzles with the help of an energy cube.

  • Development Time: 2 months.
  • Team: 5 people.
  • Game Engine: Unity.
  • Genre: Puzzle/Platformer
